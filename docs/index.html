"Wind's Wild Whispers"

**THE NEPALI POEM (रिम्जीमार्च)**

( Rimjimarch is a traditional Nepali poetic style, emphasizing a rhythm and cadence of 12 syllables per line. )

आयौ सुरु सुरु, ठाउ सुरु सुरु
काठमाडौ की धूप सुरु सुरु प्यार

सोलखली की ठंड, ठंड की छाया
आलबोर्ग महि धडकत ठंड की घाय

कोपेनहेगन महि आसरा, आसरी रात्रि
सौधारी हवा, सौधारी गुलाब कि घाम

आलबोर्ग की रात, रात की घात
रिमझिम बहार, रिमझिम घाम

काठमाडौ महि मुस्कान, मुस्कान का प्रसार
गुलाबी धूप, गुलाबी हवा, गुलाबी राम्रो

आजमानहार काठमाडौ महिता मोर
भुवन माह सुरु सुरु मोर मोर.

(Note: Please forgive the lack of diacritics and the phonetic spelling. Rimjimarch is a traditional form and difficult to transcribe into the English alphabet.)

Translation: 

"Morning's warmth, morning's warmth, a love in every place.
Kathmandu's sun, sun's warmth, a love that stays.

Aalo-Borg's cold, cold's shade, a biting frost, a cold wind.
Copenhagen's haven, a restful night, a gentle breeze.

Aalo-Borg's night, a night of gloom,
A light drizzle, a light wind.

Kathmandu's smile, spread all over,
A rose-like sun, a rose-like breeze, a joyous delight.

Let us go to Kathmandu tomorrow, my dear friend,
May the world be filled with joy, my dear friend."
