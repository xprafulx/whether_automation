But when the dawn awakens, and darkness flees,

A gentle caress, that chases away the night,
As morning's warmth, like rose petals, slowly takes its flight.

In Kathmandu's warm light, the shadows dance and play,
A vibrant tapestry, where life's vibrant rhythms sway,

Towards Kathmandu's sun-kissed hills, where love and joy resound,

**THE NEPALI POEM:**

(राम्रो पल्को र नेपालमा)

राति को कुही म्हि प्रहर नि
कैलगो को ठिङ्कनी नि तिम्रो साथि
म्हि म्हालो छै हिउँले फुल्ल
म्हि मानिसको पथ पनि के के होल

आइना को बुझो प्रहर मै जागी,
सुर्यो बेलका सुरुवा स्वाद नि लागि
आफ्नले चुम्मा के पाउने माय,
कुनै विष्या तिम्रो के फुकाले जागि

कोठा पल्को प्रहर नै देखि
तिम्रो कैलगो को भावना नि के भनि
मेरो दिल भित्रे बिश्वास राख्ले
तिम्रो साथै मै के जाऊँ भन्छले देले नि मेरो पल्को नेपाल मै जाउँ ।

(Translation:

What a lovely evening in Nepal

In the twilight, my heart whispers to you in the silence
As the snowflakes fall gently, my emotions are drawn to you
My life's path, uncertain, finds comfort in your presence.

As the morning dawns, and the sun's warmth touches me,
I find my senses awakened, and my heart filled with ecstasy
Tasting the sweetness of love, and the thrill of being alive,
I feel my spirit soar, and my soul begin to revive.

Where should I wander, lost in thought?
Your lovely presence, whispers secrets, and I am drawn to you.
My heart, in your presence, finds faith and trust,
And I, a small bird, long to fly with you to Nepal.)
