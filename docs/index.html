In whispers of Aalborg's chill,

or Aalborg's quiet mystique, where whispers speak of a love divine?
Or maybe, in Kathmandu's vibrant beat, the rhythm of the heart?

For the truth is, each place is a fragment of our soul,
a thread in the tapestry of our innermost goal,
to weave the disparate threads, the warmth, the cold, the light,
into a cloak of compassion, a mantle of humanity's sight.
