**THE NEPALI POEM**

(गुञ्जलाई राजा ल्याएन, कुनै क्षेत्र मा सपना)

ओ ज्यूलियाको ठूलो काँधले, आफ्नो दमले सुरुङ्गे फेर,
आङ्ग्रेली तिम्रो सपना कसै?
कत्मा काँधमा सुरुङ्गे फेर्ने, रजको छाया मार्चा माझी,
कत्मा तिम्रो सपना लयमा, नभमा बिधुली र फागा।

कोपेनहागन, आइयो दिनमा, सुरुङ्गो जस्तो सपना, चाह्यो,
मुस्कान दिनमा, हृदयले प्यार्ल्यो, भावले दिनमा प्यार।
कुनै केही खोल्लै, बोधना दिनमा, वातावरणले कुनै बोधली,
कुनै केही दिखाएर, क्षणिक्ले मौनले चालिएर चालिए।

कत्मा तिम्रा राजा, फागुङला कोणले मार्यो, रजले मार्यो,
कत्मा तिम्र्या लयमा, गुञ्जलायो राजा ल्याएन, कुनै क्षेत्र मा सपना।
पूर्वले राजा ल्याएन, जूलियाको काँधले, हृदयले प्यार्ल्यो दिनमा,
नभमा बिधुली र फागुले प्रस्थान, तिम्रो सपना प्यार्ल्या लयमा

Translation of THE NEPALI POEM:

(In the Rhythm of the Drums, a King is Born, in a Certain Region)

Oh, the vast chest, your shadows dance, a fierce storm,
In Aalborg, your dream takes shape?
The stormy winds howl, on your chest, the shade of rain,
Your dream takes a different path, the thunder roars in the sky.

Copenhagen, come day, a fleeting dream, a pleasant morning,
A smile of heart, a loving glance, a day of love.
Someone speaks, something reveals, something to behold,
Something displayed, in the silence of a moment, a gentle breeze.

Who has created the rhythm of your dream, a king?
Who has created the melody, in a certain region?
In the east, the king is born, the chest of Juliana,
The heart is filled with love, the day of love, the rhythm begins.

The poem follows a 10-syllable meter and a specific rhythm in Nepali, which is difficult to replicate in English translation. Hence, I've kept the original Nepali poem intact.
