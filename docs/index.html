Translation of Nepali Poem:

"The Magic of Madhu-Mas"

The sweet moon's gentle breeze, 
the moon's enchantment has descended, 
the sun of Kathmandu, so radiant, 
like a golden crown that adorns.

In the world, it seems a night in day, 
the cold wind dances, the world's asleep, 
the magic of Kathmandu, so divine, 
the world is under a sweet delusion, under my care.

The enchanting sun of Kathmandu 
is the sweet magic of my heart, the beauty of life.
The big moon of Kathmandu, a celestial delight, 
a sweet surprise in every moment, in every instant.

I would travel to Kathmandu, to relive the magic,
Under the sweet magic of Kathmandu, my spirit will soar.
In the cold breeze, I will find my own sweet peace, 
Under the magic of Kathmandu's enchanting night, my heart will dance.

In this poem, I'm inspired by the beauty of Nepal, and the magic of Kathmandu's night, under the enchanting sun. The poem, is an expression of my heart, and a reflection of the beauty of the city, and the world.
